一份惠普的报告表明，男性达到某个职位的六成要求就会申请该职位。
An HP report said that men will apply for a job if they meet only 60% of the qualifications.

而女性，她们会在100%符合的情况下才提出申请。
But women will apply only if they meet 100% of the qualifications.

这份报告一直被用来表明：女性需要更多自信和更多“安全感”。
This study is usually invoked as evidence that:well, women need more confidence and “security”.

如果换一个角度，你可能会发现，女性在这个社会里常被赋予追求完美的责任，使他们过于谨慎。
From another angle, it is rather simple to explain this:women have been socialized to aspire to perfection, which makes them overly cautious.

或许这可以解释为什么球场上会出现，技术不够精湛的男生，而女生需要很勇敢才能投出一记篮球。
And that may as well explain why males who have imperfect skills are seen on basketball court, whereas it is hard to find female players who are willing to make that single shot.
